Who I Am

About Me

Hello, my name is Devan Rao-Kathi, and I was born and raised in New York City. Growing up in NYC exposed me to a wide range of cultures, industries, and opportunities that helped shape my interests and ambitions. During my childhood, I attended The Browning School on the Upper East Side before moving to New Jersey for several years during the global pandemic. That experience helped me realize how important New York City is to me and how strongly it connects to my future goals.

I developed a strong interest in finance from an early age because both of my parents work in the financial industry. After attending a Finance and Investment Program at Columbia University, I became especially interested in private equity. I enjoy learning how businesses are evaluated not only through financial performance, but also through strategy, leadership, and long-term growth potential.

In addition to finance, I have developed a passion for real estate and architecture through my experiences in New York City. This summer, I will be interning at a New York City real estate law firm, where I hope to continue learning about business, law, and real estate development.

Outside of academics, I participate in varsity athletics and several school organizations. Through these experiences, I have developed leadership, teamwork, and time-management skills that continue to help me grow both academically and personally.
